Frequently Asked Questions

What is adaptive learning for neurodiverse students?

Adaptive learning tailors educational experiences to the individual needs of neurodiverse students, such as those with ADHD, dyslexia, or autism, by providing customized content and support.

How can I create a personalized learning plan?

You can create a personalized plan by using our platform’s adaptive algorithms, which consider the student’s learning style, strengths, and challenges to provide optimal learning resources.

What resources are available for teachers?

Our platform offers guides, toolkits, and training modules designed to help teachers implement adaptive learning in the classroom for neurodiverse students.
